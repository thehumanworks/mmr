mmr retrieve Search-to-Read Pipeline

mmr retrieve <query> turns a remembered clue into ranked prior-session context with citations and bounded message windows.

The default stays local to the linked current project. When the clue may be in another repository or harness, opt in explicitly:

mmr retrieve "sandbox image regression" --all-projects --all-sources --pretty

--all-projects searches every project discovered from local provider transcripts, not just linked Store projects. Output caps are applied after the search and ranking. --all-sources searches all supported harnesses even when MMR_DEFAULT_SOURCE is set.

Full contract: specs/retrieval.md.
